Detailed Cooking Instructions

MON: Chicken & Pepper Sheet Pan

30m ‚Ä¢ 400¬∞F
1. Prep Veggies: Cut 3 bell peppers into 1-inch thick strips. Cut onion into thick wedges. Toss in a large bowl with 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tsp dried oregano, and 1/2 tsp salt. Spread on a large baking sheet.
2. Prep Proteins (The Split):
Udit Place Extra Firm Tofu (cubed & tossed in 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tsp oil) on the LEFT side.
Maddie Cut Chicken Breast into 1.5-inch chunks. Toss with 1 tsp oil + pinch salt/pepper. Place on RIGHT side.
3. Roast: Bake at 400¬∞F for 20-25 minutes. Chicken is done when internal temp hits 165¬∞F (no longer pink). Tofu should be golden.

TUE: Roasted Salmon Bowl

35m ‚Ä¢ 400¬∞F
1. Roast Potatoes: Peel and cube 2 Sweet Potatoes (1-inch cubes). Toss with 1 tbsp oil + salt. Spread on baking sheet. Roast at 400¬∞F for 15 minutes first.
2. Add Proteins: Remove pan from oven. Push potatoes to the sides.
Udit Add Tempeh/Tofu cubes (tossed in 1 tbsp soy sauce + 1 tsp oil) to one corner.
Maddie Add Salmon Fillets skin-side down in center. Rub with little oil + lemon pepper seasoning.
3. Final Roast: Return to oven for 12-15 minutes. Salmon is done when it flakes easily with a fork. Add broccoli florets for the last 10 minutes if you like them crunchy.

WED: Creamy Ros√© Pasta

15m ‚Ä¢ Stovetop
1. Pasta & Sauce: Boil 300g Penne pasta in salted water. While it boils, heat 1 jar Marinara sauce in a large skillet. Stir in 1/2 cup Heavy Cream and simmer gently. Add 2 huge handfuls of Spinach; stir until wilted (2 mins).
2. Finish: Drain pasta and toss into the sauce.
Udit Stir in 1/2 can of rinsed Lentils/Beans into your portion for protein. Serve with side green salad.
Maddie Fry 3 slices of chopped Bacon in a small pan until crispy. Sprinkle over pasta. Serve with side Caesar (Romaine + Dressing).

THU: Steak & Dhaba Dal

25m ‚Ä¢ Stovetop
Udit (Dal Tadka) 1. Boil: Rinse 1 cup Toor Dal. Boil with 3 cups water + 1/2 tsp turmeric + 1 tsp salt for 30 mins (or until mushy).
2. Tempering (Tadka): In a small pan, heat 1 tbsp Ghee/Oil. Add 1 tsp cumin seeds, 2 minced garlic cloves, and 1 dried chili. Let sizzle 30 secs until garlic is golden.
3. Serve: Pour sizzling oil into the Dal. Stir and cover. Serve with Rice.
Maddie (Steak) 1. Prep: Pat Steak dry. Season heavily with Salt & Black Pepper.
2. Sear: Heat pan on High with butter. Sear steak 3-4 mins per side (medium-rare). Remove and let rest 5 mins.
3. Veg: Toss Asparagus in the hot pan juices for 3 mins.
