‚è≥ 45 Mins (Oven: 400¬∞F)
Ingredients:
  • Chicken Leg Quarters
  • Tempeh or Firm Tofu
  • Sweet Potatoes (Cubed)
  • Green Beans
  • Paprika, Garlic Powder

1. Prep: Toss cubed Sweet Potatoes with oil/salt. Spread on baking sheet. Bake for 10 mins while prepping protein.

Udit (Veg)

2. Cook: Roast everything together for another 35-40 minutes until chicken skin is crispy and potatoes are soft.
